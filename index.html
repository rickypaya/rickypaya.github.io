About Me

Originally, I am a Cuban American born in Miami, Florida, and one of the greatest lessons I've learned from the strong community of Cubans around me is to use what is at your disposal to solve the issue at hand. This is what has transferred to my work as a software engineer. My goal is to use the skills in my arsenal of frameworks and technologies, business strategy, and prototyping to solve the problems affecting marginalized communities through some hybrid digital-physical solution. The grandest solution I see is one that creates systems of support for those communities it provides for as it scales-as opposed to taking advantage of them- to the end of supporting the autonomy of these communities.
